Why you should adopt older cats! 🐱 💖 Created by Paige Allen

The purpose of this webpage is to show whoever ends up reading this that they should adopt an older cat at some point in their lifetime.

You know you need a cat in your life!!

Older cats are much less likely to get adopted because they can seem closed off and unfriendly. When animals go so long without love and compassion, I can't even imagine being locked up all day, when all they want is love. Older cats especially have harder times finding homes compared to kittins because people typically want a small, cute, young cat. There's nothing wrong with that, but cats of all ages deserve love! Adopting an older cat can be fulfilling because you can give that animal the most compassionate last few years of their life that they will experience.
